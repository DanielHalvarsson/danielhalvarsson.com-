Presentation av Svenska registerdata om arbetsmarknaden, företag och fastigheter

Ratio Institute · Internal seminar

Two-part presentation covering the SCB register data available in project P1463 via MONA, and a novel database of digitized annual reports built from Bolagsverket's open iXBRL data. The second part includes a proof-of-concept embedding analysis of business-condition signals in annual report text.

I SCB Registerdata — 15 register, ~680 variabler, 1990–2024 → II Bolagsverkets digitala årsredovisningar — 1,38M obs, textanalys med embeddings →
